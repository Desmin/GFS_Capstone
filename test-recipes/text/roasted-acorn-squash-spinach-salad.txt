ROASTED ACORN
SQUASH AN D
SPINACH SALAD

YIELDSZ SERVINGS

Ingredients

o 1/2 a Roasted Acorn Squash

o 1 or 2 slices of Prosciutto

o 3 cups Baby Spinach

o 2 tablespoons Crumbled Goat Cheese
o 2 tablespoons Craisins

o 2 teaspoons Pepitas

o Roasted Garlic Dressing
Instructions

1. Heat your oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment and arrange the squash
and prosciutto on top, being careful to space it out.

2. While the squash and prosciutto are in the oven, divide the spinach, goat cheese, craisins, and

pepitas between two bowls.

3. After about 12 minutes the prosciutto will have hardened and the squash will be hot. Add the
squash on top of the salads, and crumble the prosciutto over. Drizzle the roasted garlic
dressing over the top.
